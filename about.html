Our Story

About Kin Thai

A family legacy of Thai cooking, reimagined for Nantwich

From Generation to Generation

Kin Thai Restaurant was born from a passion that spans generations. Our family has been immersed in the world of Thai cuisine for decades, perfecting recipes that have been lovingly passed down from grandmother to mother, and now to us.

Building on the foundation laid by our predecessor Aroi-Thai — a beloved name among local diners — we created Kin Thai to take that legacy forward. We kept the heart of what made Aroi-Thai special: the warmth, the authentic flavours, and the dedication to every dish.

But we also wanted to evolve. Kin Thai represents a new chapter — one that embraces modern Thai dining while staying true to our roots.

Modern Thai, Authentic Soul

"Kin" (กิน) means "to eat" in Thai — and that simplicity is at the heart of everything we do. We believe great food should be accessible, welcoming, and deeply satisfying.

Our approach is what we call "modern Thai dining" — we honour the traditional recipes and techniques that make Thai food extraordinary, while presenting them in a way that resonates with everyone, whether you're a Thai food connoisseur or trying it for the first time.

Every ingredient is carefully sourced. Every dish is prepared with the same care as if we were cooking for our own family. Because at Kin Thai, you are family.
