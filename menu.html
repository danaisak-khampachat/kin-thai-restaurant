Your Safety Matters

At Kin Thai, we take food allergies very seriously. Our kitchen maintains a comprehensive Allergy Matrix for every dish on our menu.

Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary requirements when ordering. We are happy to adjust dishes where possible to accommodate your needs.
