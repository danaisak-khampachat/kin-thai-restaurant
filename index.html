Welcome

A Taste of Thailand in the Heart of England

Born from a family legacy of Thai cooking spanning generations, Kin Thai Restaurant brings the authentic flavours of Thailand to Nantwich with a modern twist.

Building on the beloved traditions of Aroi-Thai, we've created a dining experience that honours our heritage while embracing contemporary tastes — making Thai cuisine accessible and exciting for everyone.
